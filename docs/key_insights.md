# Key Insights

## Executive Summary

The refreshed Project 2 dataset represents a 2021-2025 historical synthetic procurement baseline plus January through April 2026 monthly update packages. The current reporting snapshot extends transaction activity through April 30, 2026.

## Spend And Funding

- Total procurement spend in the refreshed model is approximately **$221.35M**.
- Hardware spend accounts for approximately **$213.02M**, making hardware the primary spend driver.
- License/software spend accounts for approximately **$8.33M**.
- General Fund is the largest funding source at approximately **$124.27M**.
- E-Rate contributes approximately **$44.64M** in spend coverage.
- Grant and Federal Program funding together account for approximately **$52.44M**.

## Vendor Concentration

## Category Mix

Top categories by spend:

1. Endpoint: approximately **$106.65M**
2. Networking: approximately **$52.79M**

Endpoint and networking spend dominate the portfolio, which makes delivery speed, warranty exposure, and vendor performance especially important.
